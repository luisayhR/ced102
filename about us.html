About Us

RunTogether has been created by England Athletics, the membership and development body for grassroots athletics and running in England, to provide fun, friendly, inclusive running opportunities for everyone in England.

All our programmes, such as groups, routes, Find a Guide, #RunAndTalk and #RunAndRevise share a common theme, which is to help people to run with others. Running with others can provide motivation, guidance and support, as well as making it more fun!
